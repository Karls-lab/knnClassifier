Continuous Variables:

    age: Age in years.
    trestbps: Resting blood pressure in mm Hg on admission to the hospital.
    chol: Serum cholestoral in mg/dl.
    thalach: Maximum heart rate achieved.
    oldpeak: ST depression induced by exercise relative to rest.

Discrete Variables:

    sex: Sex of the patient (1 = male, 0 = female).
    cp: Chest pain type, with four discrete values representing different types of angina.
    fbs: Fasting blood sugar > 120 mg/dl (1 = true, 0 = false).
    restecg: Resting electrocardiographic results, with three discrete values indicating different abnormalities.
    exang: Exercise-induced angina (1 = yes, 0 = no).
    slope: The slope of the peak exercise ST segment, 
        with three discrete values representing different patterns.
    ca: Number of major vessels (0-3) colored by fluoroscopy.
    thal: A categorical variable with three discrete values representing different types of thalassemia (normal, fixed defect, reversible defect).
    num: Diagnosis of heart disease, with discrete values indicating the presence or absence of heart disease.
